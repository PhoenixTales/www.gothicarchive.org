Known Problems and Limitations

 

            - in case the character gets stuck, or is oriented diagonally after a dialog you can press DEL to correct the problem.

- If you are killed while playing, you can press DEL for a second try.

            - we've implemented preliminary english text and speech-output into this demo. Most of the ingame-text is german at the moment.

            - there are some graphical glitches and performance-problems. This alpha-demo-version might even crash sometimes, although we hope it wont. ;-)

            - compatibility-testing is not yet finished. this demo was tested on TNT2 and Geforce chipsets only.
